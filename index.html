About Wolf Pack Electronics:

Residing in Brevard County, FL, Wolf Pack Electronics is an appointment-based electronic repair business offering drop-off and mail-in services. We specialize in refurbishing, repairing, modifying, and reselling electronics to keep them out of landfills and in use. Whether you're looking to repair a device or give it new life, we’re here to help! We also livestream many of our repairs on Twitch and YouTube—stop by and watch the process live! Proudly serving our community with transparency, tech expertise, and a passion for sustainability.

Motivated by transparency, we invite our customers, and those just interested in electronic repair, to tune it to our livestream to watch our repair process live, learn a bit more about electronic repair, and/or just to say hi! 😁 Whether a seasoned engineer or technician, or someone starting out on your electronics repair journey, we welcome your presence!

Services "The Pack" Offers:

With transparency in the foreground, Wolf Pack Electronics strives to provide affordable, reliable, & high-quality repairs. Our specialties primarily reside in the servicing of Video Game Consoles & Video Game Peripherals, and when the opportunity presents itself, we also work on most electronics that come our way! Another motto of ours is, "If Electrons Flow Through It, We'll Work On It!" 🫡

Our Most Popular Services Include:

  • PS5 HDMI Port Repair
  • XBOX Series X/S HDMI Port Repair
  • PS5 DualSense Controller Stick Drift Repair
    • Upgrade Service to Hall Effect/TMR Electromagnetic Analog Sticks Available Upon Request!
  • XBOX Controller Stick Drift Repair
    • Upgrade Service to Hall Effect/TMR Electromagnetic Analog Sticks Available Upon Request!
  • Pokémon Game Cartridge Battery Replacements
    • We also Offer Save Data Back Up Services for most GB/GBC/GBA Game Cartridges!
  • Retro & Vintage Video Game Console Refurbishment
    • Console "Recap" (Replacement of All Faulty, Leaky, or End-of-Life Electrolytic Capacitors)
    • Replacement of Faulty or End-of-Life Voltage Regulators
    • Deep Cleaning
    • Shell Swaps
  • ...and Much More!

Beyond electronic equipment related to video games, Wolf Pack Electronics also works on other electronic equipment such as:

  • Lift eFoils (electric surfboards)

  • Toaster Ovens

  • ...and much more!
